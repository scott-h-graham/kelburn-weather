Method. Daily weather from Open-Meteo — ERA5 reanalysis for 2009–2025, forecast model for the in-progress 2026 edition (provisional*). Figures are the nearest reanalysis grid cell (~25 km, coastal): regional, not a gauge in the field. Sunshine-hours are omitted because ERA5 over-counts them badly; cloud cover and solar energy are shown instead.
Days. The festival is Friday–Sunday. Thursday is a bolt-on (off by default). Monday is teardown and never enters the headline metrics — it only appears at the pack-up desk. Overnight lows are the same: their own "after dark" section, never folded into the headline numbers. No festival in 2020–2021 (COVID).
Dates & types. Edition dates cross-checked and weekday-validated. Weather-type cutoffs (Taps Aff ≥22°C, Biblical ≥25 mm, Baltic ≤12.5°C, etc.) are blunt instruments for a laugh, not the Met Office. Built for the folk who keep going back anyway.
